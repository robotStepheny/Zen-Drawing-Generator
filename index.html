• Double click to start a new polygon.
  • Single click to continue the polygon.
  • Click a point to select (it will appear red), drag.
  • Points can be snapped, check for intersection (bolded point).
  • Press esc while mid-polygon to destroy.
  • Press esc while a point is selected to delete that polygon.
  • Click "Generate" to toggle the designs. You can continue to create
    polygons while the designs are being generated, but this may
    affect performance.
